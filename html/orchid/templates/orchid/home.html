Slipper Orchid Photo Identifier

Welcome to the Slipper Orchid Photo Identifier. This web application allows you to identify a photo of a slippper orchid up to the species level. Use the form below to upload your photo. The photo needs to be reasonably standardized for the identification to work properly.

Please check that your photo:

  1. has a frontal view of the flower;
  2. has only one flower;
  3. has a background with good contrast to the flower.
