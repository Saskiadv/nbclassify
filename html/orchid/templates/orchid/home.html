OrchiD

Welcome to OrchiD, a orchid photo identifier. This web application allows you to identify a photo of an orchid up to the species level. Currently, only the slipper orchids (subfamily Cypripedioideae) can be recognized. Use the form below to upload your photo.

The photo needs to be reasonably standardized for the identification to work properly. Please check that your photo:

  • has a frontal view of the flower;
  • shows only one flower;
  • has a background with good contrast to the flower;
  • is not too small (at least 400 pixel width and height).
